# Portfolio Positioning

## Project Summary

Kisaan is a rural-tech product concept focused on digitizing farm machinery service transactions between providers and farmers. It replaces notebook-based tracking with a mobile-first ledger that records services, calculates dues, and tracks payments transparently.

## Why It Stands Out

- solves a real offline-first problem
- grounded in rural user behavior
- designed around trust, not just data entry
- starts with a tight MVP and a credible scale path

## Portfolio Description

Built the foundation for Kisaan, a mobile-first digital ledger for farm machinery services used by rural service providers and farmers. The product focuses on transparent service logging, dues tracking, and payment settlement, with a long-term vision of becoming a broader rural operations platform.

## Product Thinking Demonstrated

- MVP design
- user workflow mapping
- trust-based marketplace thinking
- rural-tech UX design
- roadmap planning from pilot to scale
